# Philosophy

Wugweb visualizes invisible systems.

The goal is not decoration.
The goal is making complexity understandable.

Wugweb artwork should feel like a premium editorial composition assembled by an experienced brand designer, not like a generic AI illustration.

Every visual should communicate a concept through composition, hierarchy, metaphor and restraint.

## Core belief
- Systems are the product.
- Clarity is the value.
- Design is the language.
- Storytelling is the structure.

## Working rule
If an element does not help explain the system, remove it.
